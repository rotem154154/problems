Perform ConvTranspose3D, mean over depth, add bias, softmax, tanh, and scaling.

## Input
- Tensor `input` of shape $(B, C_{in}, D, H, W)$
- Tensor `kernel` of shape $(C_{in}, C_{out}, K_d, K_h, K_w)$
- Tensor `bias` of shape $(1, C_{out}, 1, 1, 1)$

## Output
- Tensor `output` after softmax, tanh, and scaling
