Perform Conv3D, scale, tanh, multiply, and sigmoid.

## Input
- Tensor `input` of shape $(B, C_{in}, D, H, W)$
- Tensor `kernel` of shape $(C_{out}, C_{in}, K_d, K_h, K_w)$
- Tensors `scale` and `bias` of shape $(C_{out}, 1, 1, 1)$

## Output
- Tensor `output` after sigmoid
